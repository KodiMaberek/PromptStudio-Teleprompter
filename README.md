# PromptStudio: Teleprompter

**Read Script While Recording**

Stop memorizing text before recording videos. Keep natural eye contact and never forget your lines. PromptStudio helps you record confident talking videos.

## About

PromptStudio is a simple and elegant teleprompter that lets you read your script while recording video. Your text scrolls smoothly on screen so you can stay focused on the camera and speak naturally.

Whether you're creating content, filming a presentation, or recording social media videos, PromptStudio helps you stay confident and deliver your message clearly.

## Features

- Smooth scrolling teleprompter
- Adjustable text size and speed
- Clean, distraction-free interface
- High-quality video recording (up to 4K supported)
- Works for vertical and standard video formats

## Why PromptStudio

No memorizing. No awkward pauses. Just natural, confident videos.

Designed with simplicity in mind — inspired by Apple’s design principles — so you can start recording in seconds.
